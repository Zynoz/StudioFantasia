Classical Ballet

The class

We teach using the Vaganowa technique, modernized by Kastraviskaija and Pissarev. The children are introduced to the basics of classical ballet in a playful way. Self-confidence, a sense of community and creativity are awakened through improvisation, dance combinations, rhythmic games and age-appropriate physical training. Almost unconsciously, the children get to know and understand the necessary discipline. For children from 4 years.
