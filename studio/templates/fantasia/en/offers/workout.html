Ballet Workout

Ballet elements are implemented in the training session in order to find the ideal workout for everyone. The ballet exercises are changed in such a way that even people without dance experience can easily keep up. During the lesson, exercises are done on the floor, on the stage and in the middle. The muscle groups are specifically challenged. Particular attention is paid to a healthy posture and movements that are gentle on the joints.
