About us

The class

We take time for the individual needs of our course participants. That is why we only teach in small groups.

We consciously refrain from pressure to perform. Our dancers get to know and understand the necessary concentration and discipline in a playful way. That takes more patience and effort, but it's worth it!

We are proud of the skills of our dancers and want them to show their progress and success to a wide audience. That is why we hold regular performances.

The Studio

After living in the small, fine studio in Afritschgasse for 8 years, we moved to our new studio in Wagramer Strasse 118 in autumn 2008. We would like to thank all dancers, friends and interested parties for coming, participating and stay! Located on the outskirts of Vienna, the studio is easily accessible by car, subway and bus. Underground: U1, station Kagraner Platz, bus line: 94A, station Steigenteschgasse.
