Creative Dance

The Class

Children's dance as a creative dance form brought to life through the imagination and spontaneity of children. A nursery rhyme, a song, a story, a rhythm or a certain movement becomes the subject of a dance lesson. In a playful way, the children make physical experiences that are essential for the development of their "self-awareness". According to the age, the work ranges from short sequences to concentrated dance choreographies. For children from 3 years
