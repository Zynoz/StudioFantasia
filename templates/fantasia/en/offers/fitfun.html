Fit with Fun - Gymnastics for Women

Movement sequences, conditioning training and gymnastics - adapted to your individual needs. Have fun doing it and train other important muscles in addition to the laughing muscles. During mat training we mainly work with our own body weight against gravity. The movements are carried out slowly and in a controlled manner and supported by conscious breathing, so PILATES can be started at any age.
